# Ubiquitous Language & Domain Model

## Glossary

### Sports Center (ศูนย์กีฬา)
A facility primarily dedicated to specific structured sports (e.g., football field, basketball court, swimming pool). It typically involves booking requirements or membership fees for using specific facilities.
*Categorized as `sports` in the system.*

### Recreation / Youth Center (ศูนย์นันทนาการ / ศูนย์เยาวชน)
A multi-purpose space intended for general leisure activities, open spaces, or clubs. It is generally more accessible to the public without strict facility booking.
*Categorized as `recreation` in the system.*

### Library (ห้องสมุดเพื่อการเรียนรู้)
A place for reading and learning.
*Categorized as `library` in the system.*
